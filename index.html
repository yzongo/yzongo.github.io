I’m Yannis Zongo, a third-year electronics and computer engineering student at INSA Rennes with a strong interest in how hardware, software, and data come together to solve real-world problems. I’ve worked on projects ranging from embedded systems and signal processing to data analytics and dashboard automation. Outside of class, I’m actively involved in student life as an organizer of TEDx-INSA Rennes, where I coordinate speakers and events that bring new ideas to campus. I enjoy working in teams, learning from people with different backgrounds, and building projects that are both technically solid and genuinely useful.
